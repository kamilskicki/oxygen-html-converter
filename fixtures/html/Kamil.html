The Philosophy

Theory vs Practice

Business wisdom lives in the tension between skepticism and optimism. Here's how I navigate it:

🛡️

The Skeptic

"Most AI tools are overhyped. Most growth tactics fail. Most founders chase shiny objects instead of fundamentals."

  • Ruthless prioritization
  • Data over intuition
  • Risk mitigation first
🚀

The Optimist

"AI is the biggest leverage point in human history. Bold moves create breakout success. Speed beats perfection."

  • First-mover advantage
  • Experiment aggressively
  • Think in 10x, not 10%
⚖️

The Synthesis

"Use AI to amplify what works. Test boldly, but measure ruthlessly. Move fast, but don't break the core."

  • Validated learning loops
  • Strategic AI integration
  • Sustainable acceleration
